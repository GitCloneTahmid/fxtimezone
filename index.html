Introduction

THM Limited is a private buying house operating between international apparel buyers and manufacturing facilities.

We handle sourcing, coordination, and quality oversight with a focus on execution and discretion. Our work depends on relationships built over years, not months. We represent buyers who require consistent delivery and clear communication.

Focused capabilities

Factory Sourcing

Production Coordination

Managing timelines, specifications, and communication between all parties throughout production.

Quality Oversight

Monitoring production standards and ensuring adherence to agreed specifications.
