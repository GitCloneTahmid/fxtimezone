About THM Limited

THM Limited is a privately operated buying house serving global apparel brands. Our role is simple: secure the right factory, negotiate the right price, and ensure consistent quality at scale. We do not own factories. We select the best ones.

Independent

No factory ownership. No conflicts of interest.

High Volume

Hundreds of thousands of units monthly across multiple product categories.

Buyer-Focused

We work for the buyer, not the mill.

How We Operate

1. Client Acquisition

We work directly with established apparel brands and retail groups seeking reliable, scalable manufacturing partners.

2. Factory Matching

Based on volume, fabric, compliance, and lead time, we match buyers with pre-qualified factories.

3. Price & Terms

We negotiate FOB pricing, payment terms, and production schedules on behalf of the buyer.

4. Execution

Factories handle production, logistics, and shipment. We monitor execution and communication.
